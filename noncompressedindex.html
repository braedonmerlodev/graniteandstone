M&S Welcomes You!

M&S Marble specializes in granite and marble slab fabrication/installation.

As the Bay Area's Premier Fabricator and installer of natural stone, we provide quality service to all our customers. General Contractors, designers, architects and homeowners alike, all receive the same superior service.

We can design and install quality countertops with intricate borders and backsplashes. Solid surface countertops manufactured from ZodiaqStone, CaesarStone, SileStone as well as other engineered stones. Our designers will work with you step by step to create the kitchen or bathroom of your dreams at affordable prices.
